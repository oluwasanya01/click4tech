FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

WHAT IS CLICK4TECH ?

The click4tech explorers’ is a 10-week long programme that offers job-ready training in the tech fields employers need most. It is a hybrid of online and physical classes. Click4Tech explorers’ in partnership with Berkley Academy, Juilliard Academy, Sureville Montessori school and Buttercups preparatory school, is brought to you by YAIN TECH HUB, a leading Edu-tech organization in Africa.

Which COURSE DO YOU RECOMMEND I APPLY FOR ?

The perfect programme for you really depends on your own interests. If you’re interested in coding and building websites, Front-End Web Development or Back-End Development might be for you. For those interested in manipulating large data sets or working with customer data, Data Analytics might be a good choice. Similarly, for those interested in hard ware, our robust robotics and automation program will suite you perfectly. However, for the ease of curriculum planning and structuring, we have designated three courses only for every category (age) of learners.

Can i still participate even as a student/worker?

Our programs require different time commitments to complete. So long as you are dedicated to the programs you can make it work. Additionally, for those that prefer an online session, there are no live lectures or classes to attend online. Instead, programs consist of recorded lectures, chats in online forums, and milestone-led coursework. This means that you can choose to learn at your own pace and a time that suits your schedule best.

HOW DO I JOIN YOUR SESSIONS ?

By enrolling our programme, you gain access to a world of opportunity. This includes access to our local Tech Hubs situated in both the mainland and island in Lagos. There, you can attend lectures and masterclasses, network with other tech professionals, and co-work in a state-of-the-art space. After completing your course, you will still be able to access these perks, as you will automatically be part of the click.4Tech explorers’ community, which helps learners further their career after completing their programme.

IS YOUR PROGRAM REALLY FOR BEGINNERS ?

Our programmes are designed for absolute beginners. Our Intro Data Analytics for example, will teach you the fundermentals of Data analytics. Similarly, our introduction to back-end and front-end course will teach you the basics of web development and design, starting from the ground up. All click.4 tech explorers’ programmes are beginner-friendly and do not require any previous tech knowledge.

What is YOUR REGISTRATION process like?

The click.4Tech explorers’ registration process is a two-step online process. First, applicants will be required to complete a registration form which will take a few minutes to complete. Once this has been submitted, applicants will be directed to make their fees payment, then join our online community. They will then be oriented on the program structure, and commence their program in the corresponding cohort.

WHY DO I HAVE TO PAY A REGISTERATION FEE?

The low-cost, one-time administration fee helps click4tech explorers’ hub as we continue to grow our robust online and in-person infrastructure for learners. Our city Hubs can continue to provide learners with access to state-of-the-art tech and infrastructure, providing a space for collaboration and study that is unmatched. We can also maintain our online portal to make it easier for students to access our vast tech community. The administration fee helps us keep our online portal, modern infrastructure, and career network up and running.

HOW CAN I GET IN TOUCH IF I HAVE A QUESTION

Please ask click-I our Learning Experience Assistant for more information and answers to any additional questions

AM I ABLE TO GO TO THE PHYSICAL HUBS FOR TRAINING?

Attending the in-person hubs is a benefit of the click.4Tech explorers’ experience. The in-person co-learning centers are designed to provide accountability, peer support, and the opportunity for face-to-face learning procedure.

DO I GET A DIPLOMA OR DEGREE ?

After completing each carefully designed cohort, you will receive a certificate from our issuing partner.
